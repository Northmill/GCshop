Finding a reliable seed bank matters more than most growers realize. Bad genetics waste months of effort. We've ordered from dozens of seed banks and narrowed it down to the ones worth your money.

## What We Looked For

- **Germination rate consistency** - do the seeds actually pop?
- **Genetic stability** - do plants grow true to the strain description?
- **Shipping reliability** - discreet packaging, reasonable delivery times
- **Customer support** - how they handle problems
- **Price-to-quality ratio** - premium doesn't always mean better

## Top Picks

### 1. Fast Buds

The autoflower specialists. Every strain in their catalog is autoflowering, which means they've invested all their R&D into perfecting auto genetics. Growth times are typically 8-9 weeks seed to harvest.

**Standout strain:** Gorilla Cookies Auto - massive yields, 9-week harvest, heavy potency.

**Best for:** Growers who want speed without sacrificing quality.

### 2. Seedsman

The largest selection you'll find anywhere. Seedsman carries seeds from hundreds of breeders, including their own in-house genetics. Frequent promotions and free seeds with orders make them competitive on value.

**Standout strain:** Auto Amnesia Haze - reliable, potent, quick.

**Best for:** Growers who want variety and good deals.

### 3. ILGM (I Love Growing Marijuana)

The most beginner-friendly option. Every order comes with a germination guarantee, and their growing guides are genuinely helpful. The strain selection is smaller but curated for reliability.

**Standout strain:** White Widow Auto - bulletproof genetics, ready in 8 weeks.

**Best for:** First-time growers who want confidence and support.

### 4. Barney's Farm

Amsterdam institution with decades of breeding experience. Their genetics are consistently strong and their autoflower line has improved dramatically in recent years.

**Standout strain:** Gorilla Zkittlez Auto - flavor-forward with solid yields.

**Best for:** Experienced growers looking for premium genetics.

### 5. Dutch Passion

Pioneers of autoflowering cannabis - they were among the first to develop commercial auto strains. Deep catalog with reliable results.

**Standout strain:** Auto Ultimate - one of the highest-yielding autos available.

**Best for:** Yield-focused growers.

### 6. Royal Queen Seeds

Strong European presence with competitive pricing. Their autoflower genetics are stable and they ship fast within the EU.

**Standout strain:** Royal Gorilla Auto - heavy resin, fast finish.

**Best for:** European growers who want quick delivery.

### 7. Herbies Seeds

Affordable without cutting corners on quality. Regular promotions and a wide selection of auto strains make them a solid budget option.

**Best for:** Budget-conscious growers.

### 8. Crop King Seeds

North American focus with reliable shipping to the US and Canada. Good germination guarantee and stable genetics.

**Best for:** US and Canadian growers.

## Shipping Considerations

Most reputable seed banks ship internationally with discreet packaging. If you're in the US, Seedsman, ILGM, and Crop King have the most consistent track record for getting orders through. European growers generally have faster delivery from Dutch Passion, Royal Queen Seeds, and Barney's Farm.

## How to Choose

If you're just starting out, go with ILGM for the safety net. If you want the best autoflower genetics specifically, Fast Buds is hard to beat. If you want the widest selection and best deals, Seedsman wins.

The most important thing: buy from established seed banks with verifiable reviews. The cheapest seeds from unknown sources will cost you more in wasted time than the premium you'd pay for proven genetics.
