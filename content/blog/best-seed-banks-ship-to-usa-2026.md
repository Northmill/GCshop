## Payment tips for US buyers

Most seed banks accept credit cards from US addresses without issue. If your card gets declined (some banks flag international seed purchases), Bitcoin is the reliable backup. ILGM and Seedsman both have smooth Bitcoin checkout.

Domestic seed banks like North Atlantic and Multiverse accept standard US payment methods with no friction.
